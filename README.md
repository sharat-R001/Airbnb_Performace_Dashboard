# 📊 AirBnB Global Performance Dashboard

## 📝 Project Overview
This **2-page Power BI interactive dashboard** analyzes AirBnB's market evolution from 2008 to 2020. The project transforms raw listing data into actionable insights regarding growth trends, pricing, and performance across 10 major global cities.

---

## 🚀 Key Features & Insights

### **1. Growth & Listing Trends**
* **Peak Growth**: Identified 2015 as the year with the highest volume of new listings.
* **Market Restraint**: Visualized the decline in 2016-2017 due to local regulations and the subsequent "Pre-Covid Decline".
* **KPIs**: Monitored **279,712** total listings, **182,024** unique hosts, and **144** property types.

### **2. Market Share & Pricing Analysis**
* **Revenue Dominance**: Paris, New York, and Sydney account for nearly **50% of total listings**.
* **Price Benchmarking**: Hotel Rooms lead at an average of **$800**, compared to Entire Places at **$673**.
* **Quality Metrics**: Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro are the highest-rated, while Hong Kong and Istanbul show lower average scores.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
* **Data Transformation**: Utilized **Power Query** to clean table.
* **DAX Calculations**: Created measures for `Cumulative Listings`, `Avg Price`, `Avg Rating` etc.
* **Visualization**: Built interactive line charts with annotations, Pareto-style market share bars, and heat-mapped rating matrices.
